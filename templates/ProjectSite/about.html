What Is NB Cares?

NB Cares is a multi-agency approach to wraparound services designed to improve life outcomes for New Britain residents. Its purpose is to:

1. Help residents increase and/or achieve self-sufficiency

2. Provide a searchable database of service-providers

3. Offer a community-wide calendar of family-facing events

Our Approach

Our Vision

New Britain residents will achieve positive life outcomes by increasing social and personal capital

Our Mission

A network of agencies delivering coordinated services to New Britain residents working towards increased self-sufficiency
